From this vantage point, he oversees all station operations, including the coordination of scientific experiments, the management of life support systems, and the execution of emergency protocols. Furthermore, Commander Reyes is responsible for maintaining communication with MASA ground control, providing regular updates on the station's status and receiving instructions for any necessary adjustments to the mission plan.
